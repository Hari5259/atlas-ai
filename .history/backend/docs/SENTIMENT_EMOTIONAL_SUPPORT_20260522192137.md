# Sentiment Analysis & Emotional Support Documentation

## Positive Sentiment Recognition
- Detects: happy, great, awesome, excellent, fantastic, amazing
- Responds with encouragement and enthusiasm
- Maintains positive conversation momentum
- Examples: "I'm happy", "Feeling great", "Love this"

## Negative Sentiment Recognition
- Detects: sad, stressed, down, struggling, frustrated, upset
- Provides empathetic supportive responses
- Offers assistance and understanding
- Examples: "I'm sad", "Feeling down", "Frustrated"

## Tired/Exhausted Sentiment
- Detects: tired, exhausted, sleepy, long day
- Acknowledges fatigue while staying helpful
- Offers patient assistance
- Examples: "I'm tired", "Need coffee", "Long day"
